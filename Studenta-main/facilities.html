Discover and learn how to use available resources on campus.

Central Library

Main campus library with over 50,000 books, journals, and quiet study areas.

Usage Guide
Show your student ID at the entrance. Books can be issued for 14 days.
Availability
Mon–Sat: 8 AM to 10 PM, Sun: 10 AM to 5 PM
Advanced Computing Lab

High-performance computing lab equipped with latest GPUs for ML/AI projects.

Usage Guide
Requires prior booking through the department portal. Only for 3rd and 4th year students.
Availability
Mon–Fri: 9 AM to 6 PM
Innovation Hub (Makerspace)

Workspace for hardware projects, 3D printing, and IoT development.

Usage Guide
Open to all. Consumables must be brought by students.
Availability
24/7 access with faculty approval
Seminar Hall

Large air-conditioned seminar hall with projector and audio systems for events and workshops.

Usage Guide
Must be booked at least 3 days in advance. Faculty approval required for student events.
Availability
Mon–Fri: 9 AM to 8 PM, Sat: 10 AM to 4 PM
Sports Complex

Multi-sport facility with indoor badminton, table tennis courts and an outdoor cricket ground.

Usage Guide
Valid student ID required. Equipment available on loan from the sports room.
Availability
Daily: 6 AM to 9 AM and 4 PM to 8 PM
Medical Center

On-campus health clinic with a doctor, nurse, and first-aid services for all students and staff.

Usage Guide
Walk-in allowed. Bring your student ID. Prescribed medicines are provided free of charge.
Availability
Mon–Sat: 9 AM to 5 PM. Emergency on-call 24/7
